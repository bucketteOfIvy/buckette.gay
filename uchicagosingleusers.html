Private Pissing

A Map of UChicago Single User Bathrooms

Hello! This is a map of single user bathrooms across UChicago. It is intended to be a useful resource for trans and gender-nonconforming students who just want to find a safe place to pee. However, it comes with a rather frustrating disclaimer: I cannot guarantee every bathroom on this map exists.
Similarly, the map misses bathrooms that do exist. These issues are a result of the data source for this map — the Center for Identity and Inclusion's single user bathroom list, findable at this link. I am currently working with Undergraduate Student Government to try to acquire an updated, not-misinformation-spreading list, but these efforts are going excruciatingly slow. In the meantime, I am also manually removing bathrooms that do not exist.
Even with these issues, the map is currently in a usable state, and I plan to keep improving it from here, so feel free to use it! If you do find yet another non-existent bathroom, it's likely easiest to contact me via email at wimer@uchicago.edu or to reach out on twitter (linked in the bottom right).
